IM8 and University of Oxford Begin Groundbreaking Space Experiment to Study Accelerated Aging and Longevity on-board the ISS with SpaceX
IM8 is proud to partner with the University of Oxford on a groundbreaking month-long space experiment, now underway aboard the International Space Station (ISS). Launched via SpaceX CRS-31 on November 4th 2024 at 9:29 pm EST, the mission includes sending our patent-pending Cell Rejuvenation Ingredients® (CRT8) ingredients to the ISS to study accelerated aging and longevity. By leveraging microgravity's unique environment, the experiment aims to accelerate aging processes at the cellular level and advance our understanding of longevity. Results from the space experiment are anticipated to be released in 6-8 months, offering valuable insights into longevity and cellular health.
Exploring accelerated aging in space
This study investigates how CRT8 ingredients may counteract ‘accelerated aging’ in space. By exploring zero gravity’s effects on aging, we seek insights to advance our understanding of aging and health, showcasing our commitment to innovation in nutritional science.
Why conduct research in space?
Space offers an environment that’s unique and unreplicable on Earth. Microgravity accelerates biological processes like aging and muscle atrophy, allowing scientists to study these effects in a compressed timeframe and gain valuable insights into their mechanisms and potential mitigations.

Efficacy of IM8 Daily Essentials Powder
Objective:
To evaluate the effects of the Essentials Powder formula on physical parameters, fatigue, and abdominal issues. A 12-week Randomized, Controlled, Clinical Trial Evaluating the Efficacy of a Dietary Supplement Powder Formula (Daily Ultimate Essentials) in an Adult Population.
duration:
12 weeks
Participants:
60 adults aged 25-60 with BMI 18.5-30 kg/m²
Key parameters studied:
Physical activity levels
Incidence and severity of common colds and flu-like symptoms
Blood micronutrient levels (Vitamin D, B, Folate)
Quality of Life (SF-12)
Digestive symptoms (GSRS)
Gut microbiome composition
Overall physical and digestive health
Expected outcomes:
Improved energy levels, reduced fatigue, enhanced digestive health, and strengthened immune function

Efficacy of IM8 Longevity Supplement
Objective:
To assess the safety and efficacy of the IM8 longevity supplement in improving markers of cellular health and longevity.
duration:
12 weeks

25 adults aged 40-70 with BMI 18.5-30 kg/m²

Physical function tests (6-minute walk)
Changes in waist circumference and insulin resistance

Enhanced cellular health, improved cognitive and physical function, reduced inflammation, and a potential decrease in biological age.
